Big City TKO

Congratulations to this year's TKO winners Bitchezzz (pronounced Girls)!

TKO showcases some of the best new and emerging improv troupes in head-to-head battle for the chance to be crowned Big City Improv Festival's TKO Champions!

These teams will be competing for a cash prize of $500 PLUS 25% of the door.

TKO kicks off August 16th, 2016 with a grand finale during the Big City Improv Festival in October. Each week one team will advance in the competition based on audience vote.
