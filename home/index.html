Big City Improv Festival (BCIF) is Canada’s largest improv showcase! The festival runs every October for 9 days in Toronto’s top comedy venues. BCIF features the best improvisers from across North America. In addition to hilarious shows, there are public workshops and industry panels. Stay tuned for dates and submission info for 2018.

Big City’s little sister is our TKO Tournament - an opportunity for emerging improvisers to compete for $1000 cash and 6 weeks of mentorship from a top improv coach. This competition starts every August and leads into the festival in October.
